# Brand — EMBER

_Status: active_

**Essence:** a séance, not a SaaS. Quiet, warm, a little haunted. One ember in a dark room.

## Palette
- Background: `#0c0a09` (stone-950) — near-black, warm not blue
- Foreground: `#e7e5e4` (stone-200)
- Ember (accent): `#f59e0b` (amber-500); deep: `#b45309` (amber-700)
- Muted: stone-400/500/600; borders stone-800
- The ember amber is scarce: one glowing dot, key words, CTAs, data bars. Never large amber surfaces.

## Typography
- Display: **Fraunces** (serif) — headlines, the passion's spoken lines, numerals in the Atlas
- UI: **Inter** — everything else
- The passion speaks in serif; the human speaks in sans. That contrast is the design.

## Motion
- Slow and breathing (3–4s ease-in-out pulses), never bouncy. riseIn 0.6s for entrances.
- All non-essential motion behind `prefers-reduced-motion: no-preference`.

## Voice
- Wry beats weepy. Short sentences. It misses you; it never guilt-trips.
- Microcopy in lowercase warmth: "3 minutes · no account · headphones recommended".

## Imagery
- Cinematic still-lifes: dusty objects, single warm ember, deep shadows (see assets/).
